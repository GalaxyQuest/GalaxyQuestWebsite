Knowing Your Error

Once you have your first result under your belt, now comes the task of improving the margin of error.

Here are some ways you can improve on your algorithm:


  • Play with the algorithm parameters

    • By changing the various parameters for your algorithm, you can come up with a baseline error to begin working with. These will be specific to your type of algorithm. In our case, we were able to change the max-depth of our trees, the number of trees we used, amount of features to use and the size of the subset of features each tree can choose from.

  • Test Driven Error Development

    • Create or use a method to test where your algorithm is performing weakly. By knowing where your error margins are greatest, you can develop new features to improve performance in these weak areas.

    • Avoid overfitting or underfitting

    Consider looking into boosting.
