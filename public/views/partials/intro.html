Hitchhiker's Guide to Classifying Galaxies

An Epic Walkthrough of Implementing Machine Learning Algorithms


Our Protagonist's Origins

Called into action by Galaxy Zoo Headquarters, our heroes began preparing to explore the universe, searching for signs of sentient life. While travelling, they planned to be cryogenically frozen, only to be awakened during critical moments during their mission. Knowing galaxy shape and morphological features are strong indicators of sentient life, our heroes devised a plan to train their ship's computer to classify galaxies. The more accurate their ship could become in detecting galactic features, the more fruitful our heroes expected their trip to be. Ready for adventure, they set off on their quest.
